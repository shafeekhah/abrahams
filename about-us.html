About Us

Abraham’s is the first firm in Sri Lanka to manufacture baking powder in the year 1937 after learning each and every process from the experts from foreign countries and it is said to be believed that the baking powder was only imported before. It was started in a factory situated at Hulsdorf, Colombo.

And when years passed on Abraham’s started to capture the market and it distributed to the whole bazaar and during those time Abraham’s product was distributed all across the island.

Abraham’s started introducing more food ingredients, confectioneries and bakery products. Alex toffee was manufactures and distributed by Abraham Industries and it was a very popular toffee during those time.

Currently we manufacture, import and distribute many products such as Baking powder, Custard powder, Icing Sugar, Rose syrup, Ketchups, Mayonnaise and importers and distributors of Corn flour, uncooked French fries, Potato Wedges, etc.

Vision

To be proven that Abraham’s food products is more hygienic for human consumption and expand the brand even further by expanding our market all over the island and world as it is a value added product and bring more foreign currency to our country.

Mission

To satisfy all the consumers expectation by marketing exceptionally hygienic food ingredients and confectioneries for human consumption at their affordable rates as we believe in the traditionally well known fact “CHEAP AND GOOD”.
