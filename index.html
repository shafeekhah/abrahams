Ribbon Cake

Beat Butter and Sugar until soft and creamy. Add eggs one by one. Add Vanilla and mix well. Add flour and baking powder gradually in small quantities and mix well. Add milk. Divide the mixture into three or two portions and color with pink, green, and yellow very lightly.

Ingredients:
  • 1.5 Cups of Butter
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1.5 Cups of Sugar
  • 1.5 Cups of Floor
  • 1/2 Cup Thick Milk
  • 2 leveled teaspoons Baking Powder

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

Blueberry Cheese cake

Combine crumbs, 2 tablespoons sugar and butter. Pat mixture into the bottom of a 9 inch Springfield pan. Mash cream cheese until soft and creamy. Gradually beat in sour cream, 3/4 cup sugar, vanilla and flour. Beat in eggs one at a time.

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar 1/4
  • cup melted butter 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened 1 cup
  • sour cream 3/4 cup
  • white sugar 1 teaspoon
